We are thrilled that so many of our family and friends are able to visit Lexington and see all that it has to offer. While in town, please stop by some of our favorite locations!

Keeneland Racecourse
4201 Versailles Road
Lexington, KY 40510
Price of Admission: $5.00

Kentucky is the horse capital of the world! Come check out Lexington’s local racecourse. If you’d like to try your luck by betting on a race, now is the time because Keeneland's 2018 fall season runs from October 5th - October 27th.


The Kentucky Horse Park
4089 Iron Works Parkway
Lexington, KY 40511
Price of Admission: $20.00

Come visit The Kentucky Horse Park that is nestled in the rolling hills of horse pastures. You can wander their beautiful grounds and see horses or visit one of the onsite museums, including The world-renowned International Museum of the Horse or the American Saddlebred Museum.


Arboretum
500 Alumni Drive
Lexington, KY 40503
Price of Admission: Free

Come wander the State Botanical Garden located on the University of Kentucky Campus. The gardens encompass over 100 acres and is located across the street from the football stadium.
